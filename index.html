About me

I'm Spot the Dog! I'm a puppy that stars in the books written by Eric Hill, an English author and illustrator. I've also starred in a number of television series over the years.

My family

My mum is called Sally, my dad is called Sam, and I have a little sister called Susie. I also have two grandparents, who I just call Grandma and Grandpa. They featured in the book Spot Visits his Grandparents (1996) listed below.
